\chapter*{Outline}\label{outline}}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Outline}

Literature review about the link between biodiversity facets trends and spatial/temporal scales.

The idea is to take every paper that talk about biodiversity trends (it could be either species richness, species diversity, colonization, extinction\ldots) and to list \textbf{1)} which biodiveristy metric they use \textbf{2)} which taxon/taxa they use, \textbf{3)} the spatial scale, \textbf{4)} the temporal scale and \textbf{5)} what is the dynamic (does the biodiversity metric increase/decrease/doesn't change over time).

Make a table of it and \texttt{group\_by(taxa)\ \%\textgreater{}\%\ order\_by(spatial\_scale\ \textbar{}\ temporal\_scale)}. Then see if for each taxa we can find a trend (a bit like in Chase \emph{et al.} 2019 Oikos paper \textbar{} Jarzyna \emph{et al.} 2015 but here I am not making the analysis, just taking the analysis from papers).
